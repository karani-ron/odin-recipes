Samosa recipe

Description

Samosa is a popular Kenyan street snack with a crispy, golden-brown pastry shell. It's filled with spiced minced meat, lentils, or vegetables, depending on preference. Deep-fried to perfection, samosas are savory, flavorful, and satisfying. They're commonly enjoyed as a quick bite or side with tea or sauces

Ingredients

  • Minced beef, chicken, or lentils(cooked)
  • Onions - finely chopped
  • Garlic
  • Ginger(optional)
  • Green chillies or chilli powder
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garam masala or curry powder
  • Cooking oil
  • All-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan
  2. Saute chopped onions until soft
  3. Add garlic, ginger and chillies, stir for 30 seconds
  4. Add mnced meat or lentils and cook util browned and fully done
  5. Season with salt, pepper, garam masala or curry powder
  6. Stir in chopped coriander, then let the filling cool
  7. If making from scratch, mix flour, a pinch of salt and water to form a soft dough
  8. Roll into thin sheets and cut into rectangular strips for wrapping
  9. Place a spoonfull of filing on one end of the strip
  10. Fold into s triangular repeatedly , sealing the edges with flour paste
  11. Repeat until all filling or wrappers are used
  12. Heat oil in a deep pan
  13. Fry the samosas in hot oil until golden brown and crispy
  14. Remove and drain on paper towels
  15. Serve hot with chili sauce, ketchup or tamarind chutney
